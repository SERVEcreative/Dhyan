Overview

Dhyan is a focus-training app published by Serve Creative. This policy explains what information is used when you use the Android app com.servecreative. By using Dhyan, you agree to this policy.

Information stored on your device

We store progress and preferences locally on your device (for example: sessions completed, attention index, skill track, language, and onboarding choices) using on-device storage. This data is not sent to our own servers. Uninstalling the app removes this local data.

Motion sensors

Some drills (such as Still Point) may use your device motion sensors to detect stillness during an exercise. Sensor data is processed on your device for that activity and is not uploaded to our servers.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

If you choose to watch an optional rewarded ad, we use Google AdMob. Google may collect and process information such as advertising ID, device information, IP address, and ad interaction data to show and measure ads.

See Google's Privacy Policy. You can reset or limit ad personalization in Android Settings → Google → Ads.

Donations

The app may show a UPI ID for voluntary support. Payments happen outside the app in your bank or UPI app. We do not process in-app payments.

Permissions

  • Internet — optional ads and app functionality.
  • Advertising ID — used by ad networks where applicable.
  • Keep screen on — during active exercises so sessions are not interrupted.

Children

Dhyan is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy. The "Last updated" date will change when we do. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.
