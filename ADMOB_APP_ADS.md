# AdMob app-ads.txt

AdMob requires `app-ads.txt` on the **root** of your Play Console developer website domain.

|-----|------------------|
| `https://servecreative.github.io/app-ads.txt` | Yes |
| `https://servecreative.github.io/Dhyan/app-ads.txt` | No (project subpath) |
